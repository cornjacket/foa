# Tuesday 6/23 — 150 min

This plan is made to take advantage of the full court that is available for this
practice day. The purpose of this plan is to review spacing and positioning (especially for the
block/corner position - there were a lot mistakes during our last competition),
review helping the ball carrier during transition, and leverage full-court drills
for cardiovascular work.
