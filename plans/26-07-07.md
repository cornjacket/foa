# Tuesday 7/7 — 120 min

Outdoor / concrete session. We emphasize **dribbling, shooting, and formation
practice** and deliberately avoid full-speed and scrimmaging so nobody hits the
deck — the offensive/two-man blocks stay controlled and half court. Full-court
work (dribbling warm-ups, left-handed work, press) is batched at the top while we
have the court, then we move to half court. Full-court press runs at a controlled
pace on the concrete — reads and spacing, not sprinting.

**Court availability:** dribbling warm-ups + left-handed work + full-court press
up top; everything else half court / sidelines. No full-speed transition, no
scrimmage on concrete.
